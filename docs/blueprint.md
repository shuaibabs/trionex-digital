# **App Name**: Synergy Digital

## Core Features:

- Home Page: Showcase agency capabilities, client logos, and latest blog posts in an engaging, conversion-focused manner.
- Service Detail Pages: In-depth service descriptions with scope, process, pricing, and relevant case studies, and lead capture forms.
- Portfolio Showcase: Filterable gallery of case studies demonstrating successful projects across various marketing and development services.
- Blog & Updates Section: Share expertise, industry insights, and company updates to engage visitors.
- Contact & Lead Capture: Contact form, address, phone, and email. The tool leverages generative AI to provide summaries of contact form submissions, routing each submission to the employee best equipped to answer the submission.
- Pricing Calculator / ROI Estimator: An interactive tool to estimate ad spend versus expected leads.
- Dark/Light Theme Toggle: Users can switch between a dark and a light theme for optimal viewing experience.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Indigo (#4B0082) for trust and sophistication.
- Background color: Light gray (#F0F0F0) for a clean and modern look.
- Accent color: Electric Purple (#BF00FF) to highlight key interactions.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for headlines; body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for body. This font pairing delivers a modern and readable experience.
- Use 'lucide-react' icons for a clean, consistent, and modern look.
- Responsive layout with clear sections, grid system, and strategic use of white space.
- Subtle Framer Motion animations to enhance user experience without being distracting.
